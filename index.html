Our Purpose:

Our Main Goal is to create a view for everyone that coding is not only learned by geniuses. Everyone can and should be able to learn the art of coding. Here at the Computer Science club, our powerpoint instructors provide very entertaining and easy to learn criteria while at the same time giving a bold experience of Computer knowledge. With material made from the help of a professional programmer and experienced programmers, it delivers a productive and fun learning experience for everyone.
